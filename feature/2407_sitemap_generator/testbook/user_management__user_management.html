2. Edit your own user account (publisher)

Test context
  • role: publisher
ID Action Expected Result  
BX.2.1 Log in to your publisher account    
BX.2.2 Go to Settings under My Account    
BX.2.2.1   Your account page is shown
BX.2.2.2   Your ID and email address are displayed
BX.2.2.3   You can see your user roles but you cannot edit them
BX.2.3 Modify your name and click "Update Account"    
BX.2.3.1   You receive a flash message saying your record is updated
BX.2.3.2   Your name has been updated
BX.2.4 Modify your email address to one which is already in use in the system and click "Update Account"    
BX.2.4.1   You see a warning at the top which says: Are you sure you want to change your email address? You will be logged out + will need to verify your account again.
BX.2.5 Click OK    
BX.2.5.1   You see an error message under 'Email address': Email address is already in use
BX.2.6 Modify the email address to a new one which does not exist and click Update Account    
BX.2.6.1   You see a warning at the top which says: Are you sure you want to change your email address? You will be logged out + will need to verify your account again.
BX.2.7 Click OK    
BX.2.7.1   You are logged out and are taken to the home page
BX.2.7.2   You see a message which says: Email address updated. You have been logged out for email address verification.
BX.2.7.3   You see a message which says: Debug mode - url for reset is /account/reset/39494d7f170f41439323dfa8cf8d392d
BX.2.8 Click Debug mode - url for reset is /account/reset/39494d7f170f41439323dfa8cf8d392d and set a new password    
BX.2.8.1   You are taken back to the home page and you are logged in
BX.2.8.2   You see a message 'New password has been set and you're now logged in.'
BX.2.9 Go back to Settings under My Account    
BX.2.9.1   Your new email address is stored
BX.2.10 Click "Generate a new API Key"    
BX.2.10.1   Your API Key at the top of the page is updated
BX.2.10.2   You receive a flash message saying your record is updated and the API key has changed
BX.2.11 Click "Click to Copy" icon next to your API key    
BX.2.11.1   The API key is copied to your clipboard
BX.2.11.2   Confirmation message appears and dissapears after a moment
BX.2.12 Enter mismatched passwords into the "Change your password" boxes and click "Update Account"    
BX.2.12.1   You receive an error warning 'Passwords must match'
BX.2.13 Enter matching passwords into the "Change your password" boxes and click "Update"    
BX.2.13.1   You receive a flash message saying your record is updated
BX.2.14 Click "logout" on the top right of the page    
BX.2.14.1   You see a flash message confirming that you are logged out and you are taken to the home page
BX.2.15 Click "login" on the top right of the page    
BX.2.16 Log in with your updated password    
BX.2.16.1   You see a Welcome back message
