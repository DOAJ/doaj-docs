No Downstream

A list of any entity declared either explicitly or in a relationship which does not then have other entities that it declares downstream relationships to. This may indicate that an entity is not yet fully documented, and needs further relationships defined. If not, the entity may be a terminal, in which case it can be registered in docs/featuremap/terminals.csv.
