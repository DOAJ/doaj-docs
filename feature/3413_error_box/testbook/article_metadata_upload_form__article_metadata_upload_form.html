In "Article Title" textbox enter valid article title (any string of characters)
Do not fill in any more data. Click "Add Article" button at the bottom of the page
  • Red error: 'You must provide the Full-Text URL or the DOI' appears under the URL field and the DOI field
  • Red error "Please provide at least one author" under Authors fields appears
  • Red error "Either this field or Journal ISSN (online version) is required" under "Journal ISSN (print version)" appears
  • Red error "Either this field or Journal ISSN (print version) is required" under "Journal ISSN (online version)" appears
In Authors section click on orange "Add more authors" button
  • One row for author is added
In Authors section click on one of the white "Remove author" button
  • Row disappears
Clear the form by clicking 'Enter article metadata' tab at the top to start again
Enter a title
Enter a malformed DOI
Click Add Article at the bottom
  • Red error message: Invalid DOI. A DOI can optionally start with a prefix (such as "doi:"), followed by "10." and the remainder of the identifier
Enter one or more author's name(s) of the article eg "Aga Domanska"
Click "Add Article" button
  • Error message under Authors disappears but others remain
Add author's ORCID iD in the wrong format eg "0000-0000-0000-000a"
Click "Add Article" button
  • Red error message: 'Invalid ORCID iD. Please enter your ORCID iD as a full URL of the form https://orcid.org/0000-0000-0000-0000'
Enter valid Orcid iD for one of the authors
Enter valid Full-Text URL, eg: https://pl.wikipedia.org/wiki/Torun
Select ISSN print and online version from dropdowns
  • On dropdown lists your issns are available to choose
Select 2 identical ISSNs
Click "Add Article" button
  • Errors: "This field must contain a different value to the field 'eissn'" and "This field must contain a different value to the field 'pissn'" are shown below ISSNs inputs
Choose different Online ISSN
Click "Add Article" button
  • At the top of the page you see the confirmation message: Article created/updated
