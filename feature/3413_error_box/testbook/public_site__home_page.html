Action Expected Result
Ensure you are not logged into the system
Ensure you have cleared your browser cookies (you could use an ingognito browser window)
Go to the DOAJ home page
  • The DOAJ home page is displayed
  • The DOAJ Cookie banner is displayed
Dismiss the Cookie Banner
  • The Cookie banner disappears and does not reappear on subsequent pages
Hover over Support
  • The dropdown menu appears
Move your mouse away from Support
  • The dropdown menu disappears
Click "Support" at the top
  • You are taken to Support DOAJ page
Go back to the home page
Repeat for the Apply menu
Hover over About
  • The dropdown appears
Move your mouse away from Apply
  • The dropdown menu disappears
Click "About" at the top
  • The About menu appears and stays open
Click "About" at the top
  • The menu disappears
Repeat for Search and Documentation menus
Click the orange magnifying glass, top right
  • The Quick search box appears over the homepage
Click it again.
  • The Quick search box disappears
Click Login
  • You are taken to the Login page: https://testdoaj.cottagelabs.com/account/login
Click the DOAJ logo top left
  • You are taken to the homepage
Look at the statistics under the Search box
  • The statistics show the numbers that you would expect to see
  • You can click on any of the figures and be taken to the correct list of search results
Click back on the browser
  • The number of countries is not clickable
Scroll down and click 'Why index your journal in DOAJ'
  • You are taken to https://testdoaj.cottagelabs.com/apply/why-index/
Click back on your browser and click 'How to apply'
  • You are taken to https://testdoaj.cottagelabs.com/apply/guide/
Click back on your browser and hover over the APPLY NOW button
  • It changes colour and moves
Click the button
  • You are taken to https://testdoaj.cottagelabs.com/account/login?redirected=apply
Click back on your browser and hover over the SUPPORT DOAJ button
  • It changes colour and moves
Click the button
  • You are taken to https://testdoaj.cottagelabs.com/support/
Scroll to 'From the blog'
  • There are 3 blog posts displayed
Click on the title of a blog post
  • You are taken to the appropriate post on the DOAJ blog site. Opens in a new tab.
Close the tab.
Click on "More" after a blog post
  • You are taken to the appropriate post on the DOAJ blog site. Opens in a new tab.
Close the tab.
On the right, click on 'Tweets by @doajplus'
  • You are taken to the DOAJ Twitter profile. Opens in a new tab.
Close the tab.
Click on one of the Tweets
  • You are taken to the tweet, on Twitter, and opens in a new tab.
Close the tab.
Hover over the list of tweets and scroll
  • You can scroll through 20 tweets
Scroll to the bottom and click 'Load more tweets'
  • Another 20 tweets are loaded. You can scroll up and down the list of tweets without the main scroll for the page being affected.
Click on each link in the footer, coming back to the home page afterwards if necessary
  • Each link takes you to the appropriate page
Go back to the home page
Scroll to the very bottom of the page
  • The copyright notification on the bottom right is for the current year
Click on each link.
  • Each link takes you to the appropriate page
Scroll lto the bottom of the home page. Click the orange up arrow in the bottom right-hand corner.
  • You are returned to the top of the home page
