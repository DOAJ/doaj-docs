1. Preparation

Test context
  • role: Admin
Setup:

Ensure you own a journal with ISSNs 1234-5678 and 9876-5432 and no articles attached

Ensure you are an owner of a journal with ISSN 0000-0000 that is not in DOAJ

Action Expected Result
Upload set of test articles

Test Resource: https://raw.githubusercontent.com/DOAJ/doaj/feature/3413_error_box/doajtest/xml_upload_test_package/admin_metadata_form_test_pack.xml
  • 3 Articles are uploaded and attached to the journal

2. Updating data other then DOI and Fulltext URL

Test context
  • role: Admin
Action Expected Result
Go to admin journal and articles search, choose Articles from the "Journals vs Articles" facets at the left
  • Each article in the search results has "Delete this article | Edit this article" option in the lower right corner
Find "Success 300" article
Click "Edit this article"
  • The Admin Metadata Form for "Success 300" article is opened in the new window
Check if PISSN and EISSN dropdowns have 1234-5678 and 9876-5432 options
Check that PISSN and EISSN dropdowns have not 0000-0000 option
Check the Authors section
  • There is only one author and no "Remove Author" button
Click "Add Author"
  • New empty Author Subform appears, each has "Remove Author" option
Click "Remove Author" under one of the authors
  • The author is removed from the list
Change the title to "Success 400"
Add an author filling in any string as a name, an affiliation and an orcid id in the correct form, for example https://orcid.org/0001-1234-1234-444X
Change all the other fields except doi and fulltext URL to something easily recognisable
Click "Update Article" button
  • Green banner saying "Article created/updated" is shown at the top of the page.
Search for "Success 400" article again
Close the page with the Article Metadata Form
Click "Edit the article"
  • Confirm all the metadata is updated

3. Checking data validation

Test context
  • role: Admin
Action Expected Result
Open "Success 400" article metadata form
Change DOI to any string in invalid format, eg: 0000
Change author's orcid_id to the wrong format (eg: "0000")
Click "Update Article" button
  • Error "Invalid DOI. A DOI can optionally start with a prefix (such as "doi:"), followed by "10." and the remainder of the identifier" under DOI field and "Invalid ORCID iD. Please enter your ORCID iD as a full URL of the form https://orcid.org/0000-0000-0000-0000" is shown under orcid_id field
Close the form
  • Confirm the metadata of "Success 400" article has not change

4. Change DOI and Fulltext URL

Test context
  • role: Admin
Action Expected Result
Open a "Success 400" article metadata form
Change DOI to 10.1234/200 and Fulltext URL to http://doaj.org/testing/500.pdf
Click "Update article" button
  • The red banner "Article could not be updated, as it matches another existing article. Please check your metadata, and contact us if you cannot resolve the issue yourself."
Change DOI to 10.1234/500 and Fulltext URL to http://doaj.org/testing/200.pdf
Click "Update article" button
  • The red banner "Article could not be updated, as it matches another existing article. Please check your metadata, and contact us if you cannot resolve the issue yourself."
Change DOI back to 10.1234/400 and Fulltext URL to http://doaj.org/testing/500.pdf
Click "Update article" button
  • Green banner "Article created/updated" is shown at the top page
Open the "Success 400" landing page
  • Confirm that Fulltext URL is changed to http://doaj.org/testing/500.pdf
Go back to the article metadata form
Change DOI to 10.1234/500
Click "Update article" button
  • Green banner "Article created/updated" is shown at the top page
Open the "Success 400" landing page
  • Confirm that Fulltext URL is changed to http://doaj.org/testing/500.pdf
  • Confirm only one article with DOI 10.1234/500 and fulltext URL http://doaj.org/testing/500.pdf exists
